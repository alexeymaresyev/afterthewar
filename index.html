“In my opinion, Maresyev is a hero. Despite his injuries.” -Philipp 
 After the war, Alexey lead a successful life. He 
went on to win many medals, and started training future 
pilots. His story became popular throughout all of Russia; 
schoolchildren idolized him, and he won countless awards. 
There was a book written about him (named "A Story About a
Real Man"), which was published over 100 times; there was 
even an opera created depicting his contributions to the war.

 Despite his incredible story, he received minimal 
praise outside of Russia. If you were to google his name,

like this fall into a distant memory, a memory held only by few.
